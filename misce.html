Dancing

One of the things I enjoy a lot is dancing! During my college days in Peru I was actively involved in a dance group. There I learned how to dance many Traditional Folkloric Peruvian dances.

One of my favorite dances, and maybe the most beautiful one is the Marinera, which is an elegant flirting dance. It was not until my time spent in Oklahoma, where I could actually dance it.
