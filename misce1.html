Miscellaneous

Dancing

One of the things I enjoy a lot is dancing! Nowadays, I am participating of the Ballroom Dance classes that Stony Brook gives, and during my time spent at College in Peru, I was actively involved in a dance group of the university. There I learned basically how to dance many Traditional Folkloric Peruvian dances.

One of my favorites, and maybe the most beautiful one is the Marinera, which is an elegant flirting dance. It was not until my time spent in Oklahoma, where I could actually dance it.
